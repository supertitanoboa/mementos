Mementos

Save memories that matter for those that matter.

What is Mementos?

Mementos is a mobile application which allows you to create a narrative of moments sent in a digital time capsule to someone you love.

Capture moments in the form of pictures and notes and add those moments to a timeline that a recipient will receive at a specified date in the future.
